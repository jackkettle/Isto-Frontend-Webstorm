About ISTO

This year ISTO is being held on the 27th-29th of March 2015, in the UCC Mardyke Arena in Cork city.

The Irish Student Trampolining Open (ISTO) is an annual non-profit event run voluntarily by 8 student and former student trampolinists from five universities across Ireland. Preparation begins in August and culminates in a 3 day competition, usually held in March or April. The Irish Student Trampoline Open has been very successfully running since 2002 and is now the largest trampolining competition in Ireland, and the largest student run trampolining competition in Europe.

Last year, the thirteenth anniversary of ISTO, over 500 competitors attended the competition, not including the numerous volunteer judges, score-keepers, marshals and competition organizers who helped to make the competition a great success. ISTO is attended annually by 40 different Irish, Northern Irish, Welsh, Scottish and English student trampolining clubs, and in recent years the competition has attracted interest internationally from French, Dutch and German trampoline clubs. Competitors at ISTO 2015 can potentially compete in four events: Individual trampolining, Synchronized trampolining, DMT, and Tumbling. This year, there will also be a fun club based Cheerleading event on the Sunday. The competition aims to include all ability levels, from beginners competing at novice level, to our often National level outstanding Elite competitors.
